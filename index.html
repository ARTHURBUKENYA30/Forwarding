About Us

Overseas Clearing and Forwarding (Co) Ltd was established and incorporated in 1993. Since inception, both our client base and range of services have grown steadily. We are members of UCIFA (Uganda Clearing Industry and Forwarding Association) with adequate employees qualified in customs and freight forwarding.

We provide our valued customers with the highest level of services in forwarding, customs clearance, transportation, and tax consultancy.

Our vision is to be the biggest and the best in East Africa.

Transparency, Quality, Integrity & Honesty, Teamwork, Efficiency, Mutual Respect, and Fun.

Our Services

  • Sea Freight & Air Freight
  • Long haulage services by Road & Rail (to & from Mombasa/Kampala)
  • Containerized, Bulk Cargo, and LCL Cargo handling
  • Bonded & Free Warehousing
  • Project Handling
  • Inland Distribution within Uganda

Health & Safety

Smoking is prohibited in confined areas, vehicles, storage, toilets, canteens, eating places, or near flammable materials.

Tags are required on equipment for usage and safety precautions.

Staff using intoxicants are subject to disciplinary action.

Risky and hazardous jobs are insured per the Workers Compensation Act 2000.

Waste disposal must be done properly in designated facilities.

Hazardous fluids and chemicals must meet required standards.

Compensation is provided for injuries in the course of work, subject to MD approval.

Importer’s Obligations

Importers shall avail to the agent a particular contract handling all matters relating to the services as a common point of contact. Documents required by customs include:

  • Original Commercial Invoice
  • Original Packing List
  • Insurance Certificate (if insured)
  • Original Bill of Lading
  • TIN Numbers
  • Freight Invoice
  • Certificate of Origin
  • PVOC Certificate
